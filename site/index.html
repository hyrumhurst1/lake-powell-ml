Research

Four decades of Lake Powell decline, measured from satellites preprint in prep

I reconstructed Lake Powell's water-surface area from 40 years of free Landsat imagery and validated it against the U.S. Bureau of Reclamation's measured reservoir elevation. A transparent, low-cost way to monitor reservoir loss during the Colorado River drought.
